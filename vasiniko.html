Our Story

Where the name
comes from

The word "Vasinicola" means "The Basil" in the Neapolitan dialect — an ancient term rooted in the Greek word Vazilikon, meaning "King."

Basil is a symbol of the Mediterranean: fresh, aromatic, and timeless. We chose "Vasinikò" to capture the essence of everything our pizzas represent — summer, intensity, and genuine flavour.

We are proud to be part of a celebrated tradition, with seven established Vasinikò restaurants in Italy, and now two in the heart of London.
